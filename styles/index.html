What is world of tanks?

WOT BLITZ is a free-to-play Massively Multiplayer Online Game developed and published by Wargaming.net for devices running iOS, Android, Windows, macOS and Nintendo Switch. The game was originally released to a limited audience in selected countries on May 5, 2014, and was officially released on June 26, 2014. World of Tanks Blitz almost completely repeats the gameplay of World of Tanks and includes many elements of the original game. The game received positive reviews from critics and players. The free-to-play system, the correct adaptation of the “original” to the needs of the platform, gameplay and graphics were most highly rated, but the touch controls were criticized.
